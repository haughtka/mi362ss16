My Collection of Travels


A Gray Day In Cinque Terre

Cinque Terre is a very popular italian tourist location. Its brightly colored terraces mixed with the rugged steep landscape and sharp cliffs overlooking the sea make it a photographer's dream. Where most people are cluttered around that paths that offer a view of the colorful buildings overlooking the water, I decided to venture off and found appreciation for the gray side of the island.

Super Blood-Moon Lunar Eclipse On A Starry Night

On September 27th, 2015 much of the world witnessed the phenomenon of a super blood moon/total lunar eclipse. This combination only occurs once every few decades. The last one was documented in 1982, and the next wont happen until 2033. I'm happy I was able to get out and enjoy the experience while snapping a few good pictures in the process.

The Bubbling Sulfer Pits Of Wai-O-Tapu, New Zealand

New Zealand travel is famous for its breathtaking South Island. However, the North Island has a few treasures of its own. One of those treasures is the Wai-O-Tapu Geothermal Park located in Roturoa. This photo is one of my absoulte favorites due to its brilliant colors and excellent display of bubbling geothermal activity.
